## 1️⃣ PREPROCESS TEXT BEFORE AI (CRITICAL)

Before sending text to AI:

Clean it.

### Remove common CV section headers:

```ts
const cleanText = rawText
  .replace(/\b(CONTACT|SKILLS|EDUCATION|EXPERIENCE|PROFILE|SUMMARY|LANGUAGES|CERTIFICATIONS)\b/gi, "")
```

Also:

* Remove excessive line breaks
* Normalize spaces

```ts
.replace(/\n{2,}/g, "\n")
.replace(/\s{2,}/g, " ")
```
